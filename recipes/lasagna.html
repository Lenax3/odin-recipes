Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian layered pasta dish with rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and flavorful spices. It’s perfect for family dinners and gatherings.

This recipe combines traditional flavors with fresh ingredients to create a hearty and delicious meal everyone will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 700 ml tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 250 g ricotta cheese
  • 300 g mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 50 g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F). Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions and drain.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  3. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Add a layer of noodles, then ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  7. Top with the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 35–40 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden brown.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.

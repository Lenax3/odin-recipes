Kitsune Udon

Description

Kitsune Udon is a traditional Japanese noodle soup featuring thick udon noodles in a flavorful dashi broth.

Topped with seasoned fried tofu, spring onions, and narutomaki, it's a simple yet comforting meal with authentic Japanese flavors.

Ingredients

  • 2 portions frozen or fresh udon noodles
  • 2 cups dashi stock
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 pieces seasoned fried tofu (aburaage)
  • 2 narutomaki slices
  • 2 tbsp chopped spring onions
  • 1 tsp shichimi togarashi (optional)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dashi broth by combining dashi stock, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar in a saucepan. Bring to a gentle simmer.
  2. Heat the seasoned fried tofu (aburaage) in the broth for 2–3 minutes until warmed through.
  3. Cook the udon noodles according to the package instructions, then drain well.
  4. Divide the cooked udon noodles between two serving bowls.
  5. Pour the hot broth over the noodles.
  6. Top each bowl with the seasoned fried tofu, narutomaki slices, and chopped spring onions.
  7. Sprinkle with shichimi togarashi if desired and serve immediately while hot.
